TOURIST SPOTS

10K ROSES

10k roses is one of the tourist spots here in Cordova, it is located at the end part of Day-as Cordova,Cebu. It is said that it has 10,000 artificial flowers that are color white but it glows in the dark.

HISTORY

Cebu-Cordova Link Expressway

The Cordova-Cebu Link Expressway (CCLEX) is evidence of the Philippines' dedication to the development of contemporary infrastructure. This massive project offers improved connectivity and economic prosperity for the region as it establishes a key link between the thriving municipalities of Cordova and Cebu.
